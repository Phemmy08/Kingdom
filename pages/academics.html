Curriculum & Academics

Curriculum overview for Kingdom Life International Academy Nigeria (KLIAN)

Junior Secondary School (JSS1 - JSS3)

Our Junior Secondary School adheres strictly to the Nigerian 6-3-3-4 National Policy on Education, providing a strong foundation in essential subjects.

It is Designed to create an engaging and holistic learning environment. Teachers at this level are dedicated to understanding each students needs to foster personal growth and academic excellence. The programme includes:

Core Subjects:

  • Mathematics
  • English Language
  • Integrated Scrience(Covering basic principles of Biology, Chemistry and Physics)
  • Social Studies
  • Physical and Health Education
  • Basic Technology (Including ICT)
  • French Language
  • Cultural and Creative Arts (Including music, Dance and Drama)
  • Pre-Vocational Studies (Agricultural Science and home Economics)
  • Business Studies
  • Religion and National Values (Including Civic Education and Security Education)
  • Yoruba (or another Nigerian Language)

Students will experience a dynamic mix of academics, sports and arts, enriched with field trips and Interactive learning activities. Individualized plans help each students build a solid foundation for their Progression into Secondary School.
