Our Story

Kingdon Life International Academy Nigeria (KLIAN) is a distinguished educational institute in Africa, dedicated to the dual mandate of raising the foundation of many generations, both academically and morally. As a non-co-educational school, KLIAN is committed to creating a dynamic learning environment where students are inspiredto explore, innovate and colaborate. We foster a culture of critical thinking, empathy and Global Citizenship, preparing our students to make meaningful impacts on the world.

KLIAN was established in the response to the pressing need for high-quality, comprehensive education amidnst the challenges facing the Nigerian educational sector. Located at P.O. Box 1 Agbasa, Ikere Road, Ijare, Ondo State, KLIAN Emerged from a vision to address the decline in robust educational practices by offering solid academic foundations for future generations and imculating in them the right mental drive and Christian Values from their Formative ages.

The academy builds on the success of its university foundation programs, where it has achieved notable academic excellence, with over 90% success rates. This experience has inspired KLIAN to extend commitment to educational excellence beyond universitie preparation, now serving students at the Junior Secondary School level (JSS1 and JSS2).

Founded by Pst. Mayowa Akinola, who also serves as the chairman of the Kinmayor Leadership Foundation - a non governmental Organisation established in 2014 and active in Lagos and Ondo State - KLIAN reflects his deep belief in the potential of Africa's Next generation. Through this acadeny, Pst. Akinola aims to contribute significantly to reshaping the educational landscape and empowering young minds to drive positive change accross the continent.
